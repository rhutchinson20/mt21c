Tonic prolongation function (abbreviated “ton. prol.”): The \(\left.\text{vi}\right.\) and \(\left.\text{iii}\right.\) (chords that share two common tones with \(\hat{1}\) –\(\hat{3}\) –\(\hat{5}\) from the tonic triad) tend to occur after the tonic chord and progress to chords of pre-dominant function.
