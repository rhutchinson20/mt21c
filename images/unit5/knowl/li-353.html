Item 2.c.

The plus sign (\(\left.\text{}{+}\right.\)) can mean “augmented triad” or is equivalent to a sharp when placed in front of 9, 11, or 13. C\(\left.\text{}{+}\right.\)7 means a C augmented triad (“C\(\left.\text{}{+}\right.\)”) with a minor seventh from the root to the seventh(“7”), not a C major triad with a major 7th.
